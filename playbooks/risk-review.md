# Playbook: Risk Review

Use this playbook to conduct a structured risk review at any project milestone.

## Steps

1. **List all known risks** — Don't filter yet. Get everything on the table.
2. **Assess each risk** — Likelihood (High/Med/Low) and Impact (High/Med/Low).
3. **Score and rank** — High×High = Critical. Prioritize accordingly.
4. **Assign mitigation actions** — For every open risk, there must be a mitigation action and an owner.
5. **Set review dates** — When will each risk be reassessed?
6. **Close resolved risks** — Document how they were resolved.
7. **Escalate critical risks** — Critical risks need executive awareness.

## Risk categories to check

- Scope
- Resource / capacity
- Technical / feasibility
- Timeline / external dependencies
- Communication / stakeholder alignment
- Compliance / regulatory

## Output

Use `templates/en/risk-register.md`.
