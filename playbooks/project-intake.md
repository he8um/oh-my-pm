# Playbook: Project Intake

Use this playbook when starting a new project engagement.

## Steps

1. **Clarify the problem** — What is the business problem? Who is affected?
2. **Identify goals** — What does success look like? What metrics will we track?
3. **Define scope** — What is in scope? What is explicitly out of scope?
4. **Map stakeholders** — Who is the executive sponsor? Who are the decision makers? Who is impacted?
5. **Understand constraints** — What are the budget, resource, and timeline constraints?
6. **Identify dependencies** — What external teams, systems, or decisions does this depend on?
7. **Surface initial risks** — What could go wrong before we even start?
8. **Define the first milestone** — What is the first concrete deliverable?
