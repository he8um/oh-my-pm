# Playbook: Launch Readiness

Use this playbook to assess readiness for a product or campaign launch.

## Readiness checklist

### Product / Engineering

- [ ] Features complete and tested against acceptance criteria
- [ ] QA sign-off received
- [ ] Performance tested at expected load
- [ ] Security review complete
- [ ] Rollback plan documented and tested
- [ ] Monitoring and alerting in place
- [ ] Support team briefed

### Marketing / Communications

- [ ] Launch messaging approved
- [ ] Assets complete and reviewed
- [ ] Press / outreach materials ready (if applicable)
- [ ] Social media scheduled
- [ ] Internal communications sent

### Operations

- [ ] Customer support trained and ready
- [ ] Documentation published
- [ ] Known issues and FAQs prepared

### Decision

- [ ] Go/no-go decision made and documented
- [ ] Decision owner identified
- [ ] Escalation path defined if go/no-go criteria not met

## Output

Document the go/no-go decision in `templates/en/decision-log.md`.
