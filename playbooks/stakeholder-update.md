# Playbook: Stakeholder Update

Use this playbook to prepare a stakeholder update.

## Steps

1. **Identify audience** — Who is receiving this update? Executive, team, cross-functional?
2. **Lead with status** — RAG status with a one-line rationale.
3. **Summarize what changed** — Not everything — what changed since the last update.
4. **Flag decisions needed** — What requires a decision from the reader? Be explicit.
5. **Surface risks** — Top 1-2 risks only. Don't bury the reader.
6. **State next milestones** — Dates and owners.
7. **Close clearly** — What action, if any, does the reader need to take?

## Audience calibration

| Audience | Focus |
|---|---|
| Executive | Status, decisions, risks, timeline/budget impact |
| Cross-functional team | Shared milestones, dependencies, what you need from them |
| Engineering team | What to build, blockers, escalation path |

## Output

Use `templates/en/status-report.md`.
