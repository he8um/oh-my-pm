# Playbook: Scope Control

Use this playbook when scope is expanding, unclear, or at risk.

## Steps

1. **Define the original scope** — What was agreed? Where is it documented?
2. **Identify scope additions** — What has been added since the project started?
3. **Assess impact** — What does each addition cost in time, resource, and risk?
4. **Categorize additions** — Must have now / Should have now / Defer / Remove.
5. **Document the decision** — Record what was decided, why, and by whom.
6. **Communicate the change** — Brief stakeholders on the impact and decision.

## Signs of scope creep

- Requirements growing without timeline or resource adjustment
- "While we're at it..." requests from stakeholders
- Stories or features added after sprint planning
- PRD being updated after sign-off without a formal change process
