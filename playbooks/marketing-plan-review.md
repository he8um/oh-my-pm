# Playbook: Marketing Plan Review

Use this playbook to review a marketing or campaign plan.

## Steps

1. **Goal clarity** — Is the campaign goal specific and measurable?
2. **Audience** — Is the target audience defined? Is the messaging appropriate for them?
3. **Channels** — Are channels selected based on where the audience is? Is the mix justified?
4. **Timeline** — Is the timeline realistic? Are asset production timelines included?
5. **Asset readiness** — What assets are needed? What is the production status?
6. **Dependencies** — What depends on product readiness, legal approval, or other teams?
7. **Risks** — Designer bandwidth, platform algorithm changes, product delays?
8. **Budget** — Is it allocated? Is there a contingency?
9. **Measurement** — How will success be measured post-launch?
