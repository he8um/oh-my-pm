# Playbook: Delivery Planning

Use this playbook to create a structured delivery plan for an initiative.

## Steps

1. **Understand the goal** — What does done look like? What is the success metric?
2. **Break down the work** — What are the major milestones? What are the tasks within each?
3. **Identify dependencies** — What must happen before what? What is external?
4. **Map the critical path** — Which sequence determines the earliest completion?
5. **Assign ownership** — Who is responsible for each milestone and task?
6. **Set target dates** — Realistic, not aspirational. Buffer for known risks.
7. **Document risks** — Top 3 risks with mitigation and owner.
8. **Define go/no-go criteria** — What must be true to proceed at each major milestone?

## Output

Use `templates/en/roadmap.md` and `templates/en/risk-register.md`.
