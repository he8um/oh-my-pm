# Playbook: PRD Review

Use this playbook to review a PRD before engineering begins.

## Steps

1. **Problem statement** — Is it specific? Does it identify the user and the pain?
2. **Goals** — Are they measurable? Do they connect to a business outcome?
3. **Scope** — Is it defined? Are non-goals listed?
4. **User stories** — Do they cover the key scenarios? Are edge cases considered?
5. **Acceptance criteria** — Is every requirement testable? Who signs off?
6. **Dependencies** — Are all external dependencies identified with owners?
7. **Risks** — Are technical and product risks documented?
8. **Success metrics** — Is there a target? A measurement method?
9. **Open questions** — Are unresolved decisions tracked with owners and dates?

## Common PRD gaps

- No acceptance criteria
- Goals without targets (e.g., "improve performance" — by how much?)
- No non-goals
- Missing dependencies
- Success metric defined but not measurable
