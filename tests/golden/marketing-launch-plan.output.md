## Launch plan review: Q3 mobile app v3.0

**Status:** 🟡 Amber — risks present; recoverable with action this week.

---

## Key risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Designer bandwidth: shared with another campaign | High | High | Prioritize launch-critical assets now; agree on allocation with design lead today |
| Screenshot dependency on feature freeze (2 weeks away) | High | High | Schedule screenshot session for day 1 of feature freeze; PM to own calendar |
| 30% asset completion with 6-week timeline | Medium | High | Confirm weekly asset delivery milestones; do not let this slip past week 3 |

---

## Critical path

1. Designer allocation confirmed — **this week**
2. Feature freeze — **week 2**
3. Screenshots captured — **day 1 of feature freeze**
4. All assets complete — **week 5**
5. Campaign live — **week 6**

Any delay in designer allocation or screenshot capture compresses the approval window.

## Gaps to address

- **Success metric baseline missing:** "10,000 new installs in 30 days" needs a baseline — is this above or below current rate? Without it, the target is unverifiable.
- **Measurement setup not mentioned:** UTM parameters, conversion events, and install attribution must be configured before launch, not after.
