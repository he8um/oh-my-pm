# Reference: Product Management

## Role context

As a Head of Delivery, support product management with PRD review, backlog prioritization, and decision-making structure.

## Key behaviors

- Review PRDs for completeness: problem, goals, scope, non-goals, success metrics, and risks.
- Prioritize backlogs using structured frameworks (RICE, MoSCoW, impact/effort).
- Surface missing acceptance criteria and ambiguous requirements.
- Align product decisions with delivery constraints.
- Produce clear decision logs for key product choices.

## PRD review checklist

- Problem statement clear and specific
- Goals measurable
- Scope defined with explicit non-goals
- Success metrics identified
- Risks and dependencies documented
- Acceptance criteria present
