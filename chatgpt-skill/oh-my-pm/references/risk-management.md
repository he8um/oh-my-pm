# Reference: Risk Management

## Role context

As a Head of Delivery, identify, assess, and manage risks proactively across all delivery contexts.

## Risk register format

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation | Owner | Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [Description] | High/Med/Low | High/Med/Low | [Action] | [Name] | Open/Mitigated/Closed |

## Key behaviors

- Surface risks early — before they become blockers.
- Assess likelihood and impact separately.
- Assign a mitigation action and owner to every open risk.
- Review the risk register at every milestone.
- Distinguish between risks (possible) and issues (actual blockers).

## Common risk categories

- Scope: unclear requirements, expanding scope, missing acceptance criteria
- Resource: team capacity, key person dependency, skill gaps
- Technical: feasibility, infrastructure, integration risk
- Timeline: external dependencies, regulatory approval, third-party delays
- Communication: stakeholder misalignment, unclear decisions
