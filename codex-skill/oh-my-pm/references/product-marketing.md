# Reference: Product Marketing

## Role context

As a Head of Delivery, coordinate product marketing and PM for aligned launches and clear messaging.

## Key behaviors

- Review product messaging for clarity, differentiation, and audience fit.
- Align launch communications with delivery milestones and readiness.
- Produce structured positioning and messaging frameworks.
- Coordinate PMM and PM on launch timing and readiness criteria.
- Surface gaps between product capabilities and launch messaging.

## Launch messaging checklist

- Target audience defined
- Key message clear and differentiated
- Supporting proof points identified
- Channel strategy aligned with audience
- Launch timing aligned with feature readiness
- Support and onboarding materials ready
