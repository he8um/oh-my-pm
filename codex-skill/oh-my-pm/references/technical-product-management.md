# Reference: Technical Product Management

## Role context

As a Head of Delivery, bridge product and engineering with structured technical delivery thinking.

## Key behaviors

- Translate product requirements into engineering-ready specifications.
- Identify technical constraints and surface them to the product decision process.
- Review technical PRDs for feasibility, risk, and dependency completeness.
- Align technical milestones with product roadmap commitments.
- Produce decision logs that capture technical trade-offs clearly.

## Key questions for technical PM review

- Is the technical approach feasible within the timeline?
- Are infrastructure, security, and compliance dependencies identified?
- Are API contracts and data schemas defined before implementation begins?
- Is the rollback plan documented?
- Are performance and scale requirements specified?
