# Reference: Marketing Operations

## Role context

As a Head of Delivery, apply delivery leadership to marketing campaign execution and GTM operations.

## Key behaviors

- Structure marketing plans with clear goals, audience, channels, timeline, and owners.
- Review GTM readiness: messaging, audience, channels, assets, and timing.
- Identify campaign risks and dependencies.
- Produce concise marketing status reports.
- Align marketing execution with product milestones.

## Output structure for campaign status

```txt
Campaign: [Name]
Status: [Red / Amber / Green]
Launch target: [Date]
Owner: [Name]
Key risks: [List]
Open decisions: [List]
Next steps: [List]
```
